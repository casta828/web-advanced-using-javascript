pacha = earth

Farmer Driven Food Catalog



pacha is a food catalog designed to presever farmers' rights, indigenous knowledge and protect their biodiversity.



pacha looks to empower farmers through education by creating small social networks and online communities, which let farmers share their knowledge of foods, farming techniques or personal stories working their land.
